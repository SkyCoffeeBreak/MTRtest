Our Services

Wholesale, Commercial & Residential

At Metal Tile Roof LLC, we provide high-quality metal roofing solutions for wholesale, commercial, and residential needs. Our products are made with American-made steel, available in 24 gauge.

Kynar Coated Colors Available

Choose from a variety of flat and metallic colors, all Kynar coated for exceptional durability. Our standard offering includes a 35-year warranty, with a gold warranty available for added peace of mind.

Product Options

  • Corrugated Metal Tile Roofing: Our corrugated metal tile roofing ensures easy installation and a long-lasting solution. Perfect for any project size with roof panels available up to 40' in length.
  • Woodzac Screws: Available in all colors to match your roofing, ensuring a seamless and professional finish.
